Lodging

The Homewood Suites by St. Louis Riverport Airport West is about 5 minutes from the fields. We reserved several King suites which also have a pull out couch in the living room and a full kitchen. They also serve breakfast and have WIFI. Rooms are $112 per night. When you call just let them know you are making a reservation for the USAU Sectionals ultimate tournament hosted by Wash U.
