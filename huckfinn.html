Huck Finn 2017

April 1-2, 2017

St. Louis, MO

Wash U Contra is excited to be partnering with Spin Ultimate to host the 21st annual Huck Finn, the Midwest's premier college open tournament. Right before the post season, what better way to sharpen your teeth than with the best teams in the midwest, including reigning champions, Minnesota Grey Duck! For our 21st bash, we're pulling out all the stops. Teams at Huck Finn can expect tough regional and out-of-region competition, power pools, high quality fields, sweet gear from Spin, and all your favorite tournament goodies. If you have any questions please contact us at contra.huckfinn@gmail.com. We are looking forward to hearing from you! Stay tuned for more details about the tournament as the date approches.
