About Us

Contra is Washington University’s Men’s Club Ultimate Frisbee team. Founded in 1987, Wash U Ultimate was renamed Contra in 2002. We travel to 4-5 tournaments in the fall and 6 tournaments in the spring, including the USA Ultimate College Championship Series. Contra is composed of 31 players committed to challenging ourselves and constantly improving to become the best team we possibly can. At the same time we embrace and cherish the fun-spirited, goofy culture of the sport and are united by a common love and passion for the game. We host tryouts every fall and welcome everyone who expresses interest. Previous experience is not required in order to make the team. That being said, we have found that skills from many team sports such as soccer, basketball, football, etc. translate well onto the Ultimate field.
